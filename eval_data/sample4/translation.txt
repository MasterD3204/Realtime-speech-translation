From then on, Hanoi has continuously risen and developed strongly. On the first of August in the year two thousand and eight, Hanoi officially expanded its administrative boundaries, merging the entire Ha Tay province, Me Linh district of Vinh Phuc province, and four communes of Luong Son district, Hoa Binh province. This event increased the capital's area by three point six times, reaching over three thousand three hundred and forty square kilometers, making Hanoi one of the seventeen largest capitals in the world by land area.
