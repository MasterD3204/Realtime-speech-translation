Going back in time to the autumn of the year one thousand and ten, the wise King Ly Thai To issued a royal decree to move the capital, deciding to transfer it from Hoa Lu to Dai La and renaming it Thang Long, which means ascending dragon. Since October of that year, this land has witnessed countless ups and downs in our nation's history. Throughout its development, Hanoi has gone through various names such as Dong Do, Dong Quan, or Bac Thanh. It was not until the year one thousand eight hundred and thirty-one that King Minh Mang of the Nguyen Dynasty officially named this province Hanoi, which means the land situated within the rivers.
