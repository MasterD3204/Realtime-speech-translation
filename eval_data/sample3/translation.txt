Through two protracted resistance wars against the French colonialists and the American imperialists, the army and people of Hanoi demonstrated a resilient and indomitable spirit. Specifically, the "Dien Bien Phu in the Air" victory, which took place in December of the year one thousand nine hundred and seventy-two, left a profound mark on world history. The tenth of October in the year one thousand nine hundred and fifty-four was the capital's liberation day, marking a brilliant milestone in the golden historical pages of the nation.
